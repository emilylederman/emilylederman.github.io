About Me

My name is Emily Lederman. I am a third year computer science major at the Rochester Institute of Technology, where I am cross-registered with the National Technical Institute for the Deaf. Currently, I am on co-op with Intuit in sunny San Diego.

I was born and raised in Morgantown, WV. At 18, I moved to Santa Cruz, California with my family. At a young age, I developed a lifelong love of computing and robotics, as well as a passion for art and swimming. As of this spring, I taking a medical redshirt from my junior season as a varsity swimmer for RIT after two successful seasons in the pool. In my spare time, I enjoy artistic pursuits, cooking, and weightlifting.

I put together this website to showcase my work and hobbies. Please feel free to contact me if you would like to get in touch!
